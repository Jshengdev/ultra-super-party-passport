The Ultra Super Social Passport

Imagine walking into a room of 193 strangers — and knowing exactly which two you came to meet.

The problem

Every event you've ever been to was curated — you just never got to see it.

Events sell the lineup, the venue, the ticket — never the people.

A list of 200 guests says “software engineer” two hundred times.

And all the should-I-go-talk-to-them math happens alone, in your head.

What we're doing

Sign up to a party.
A passport writes itself.

The guest list becomes a relational graph — school, craft, and what you believe.

An agent walks your paths and writes your passport: the two people to find tonight and why, a hidden mission, and one line about you it was never told.

Relational, not a knowledge graph. The AI is invisible — the room just knows you.

How it works

One CSV in. Every claim receipted.

BUTTERBASEThe backend of record — signups & passports tables, the AI gateway every model call routes through, the live deploy, and the MCP the submission itself travels on.
NEO4J AURAThe ontology — typed people, schools, crafts, value-clouds; provenance stamped on every write; the agent traverses typed paths, never freeform.
ROCKETRIDEThe deployed pipeline — the inference path runs as a managed cloud endpoint the app calls.
THE PASSPORTThe audit — every “why” on every passport resolves to real edges in the live graph. Receipts, or it doesn't ship.
